Section D: Correctional Service Specific
41. What is the main purpose of correctional facilities?
Answer: Rehabilitation and reformation of offenders
42. Who is an inmate?
Answer: A person confined in a prison or correctional facility.
43. What is parole?
Answer: Conditional release of a prisoner before completion of sentence under
supervision.
44. What is recidivism?
Answer: Returning to crime after being released from prison.
45. Who is a warder?
Answer: A correctional officer or prison guard.
46. Types of correctional facilities in Nigeria?
Answer: Custodial centres and non-custodial units
47. Examples of non-custodial measures?
Answer: Community service, parole, probation
48. What is probation?
Answer: An alternative sentence allowing an offender to remain in the community under
supervision.
49. What is bail?
Answer: Temporary release of an accused person awaiting trial.
50. What is solitary confinement?
Answer: Keeping an inmate alone in a cell as punishment.
